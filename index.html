Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between SVG and PNG formats?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is a vector format that can be scaled to any size without losing quality. It's ideal for responsive designs and print materials. PNG (Portable Network Graphics) is a raster format that has a fixed resolution. We provide PNGs in multiple sizes for different use cases.

Can I use these logos for commercial projects?

Please refer to the original repositories for licensing information. We provide easy access to these logos but do not own the rights to them. Most cryptocurrency logos are subject to their respective trademark policies.

How often are new logos added?

Our logo collection is regularly updated as new cryptocurrencies gain popularity. We prioritize adding logos for projects listed on major exchanges and ranking sites.

I can't find a specific cryptocurrency logo. What should I do?

If you can't find a specific logo, you can contribute to the original repositories by submitting a pull request with the missing logo.
